## 2025-04-30 - Chat Composer Accessibility
**Learning:** In a chat composer interface, icon-only buttons like 'Attach file', 'Toggle link', 'Voice input', and 'Send' can be very confusing for screen reader users if missing aria-labels. Adding titles also helps visual users decipher icons quickly via tooltips.
**Action:** Always add both aria-label and title attributes to all icon-only buttons to enhance accessibility and usability across the board.
