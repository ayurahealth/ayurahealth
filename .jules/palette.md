## 2024-05-24 - Accessibility for Icon-Only Buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (e.g., `Paperclip`, `Mic`, `LinkIcon`) in ChatComposer lacked `aria-label` and `title` attributes, severely limiting screen reader accessibility and hiding helpful tooltips from mouse users.
**Action:** Consistently add descriptive `aria-label` and `title` attributes to all icon-only interactive elements to ensure compliance with a11y standards and improve the user experience.
