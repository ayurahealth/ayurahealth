## 2026-04-30 - Prevent O(N) Re-renders in Message List during Streaming\n**Learning:** In Next.js App Router client components, complex list items (like chat messages rendering Markdown with `ClinicalMarkdown`) need `React.memo()` alongside memoized handlers (`useCallback`) to avoid expensive, unnecesary re-renders of the entire array on every stream tick.\n**Action:** Wrapped `MessageItem` with `React.memo` and extracted its callback props to stable `useCallback` references in the parent component.
