# AyuraHealth iOS App Store Submission Pack

## 1) App Store Listing Copy

### App Name
AyuraHealth

### Subtitle
Ancient wellness, guided by AI

### Promotional Text
Educational wellness guidance inspired by Ayurveda, Chinese Medicine, and other traditional systems. Personalized habits, routines, and wellness insights in one place.

### Description
AyuraHealth is an educational wellness app that helps you explore traditional wellness frameworks with modern AI assistance.

What you can do:
- Ask wellness questions and receive concise guidance
- Explore habits, routines, and lifestyle recommendations
- Use tradition-focused views (Ayurveda, TCM, and more)
- Track your wellness journey in a simple chat interface

Important:
AyuraHealth is for educational wellness purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis, treatment, or emergency care.
