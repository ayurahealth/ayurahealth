# AyuraHealth Growth & Marketing Playbook

## North-Star Goal (Next 90 Days)
Build trusted top-of-funnel for educational wellness and convert high-intent visitors into first consultations.

## 1) Channel Strategy

### SEO
- Publish 2 educational pages/week (symptom cluster + habit guidance)
- Target intent clusters: stress, sleep, digestion, fatigue, skin, immunity
- Every page includes:
  - clear educational disclaimer
  - internal links to `chat`, `pricing`, `safety`
  - FAQ structured content

### Social (Organic)
- Instagram/Reels: short practical tips (3/week)
- LinkedIn: founder-led credibility posts (2/week)
- YouTube Shorts: symptom-to-routine quick explainers (2/week)

### Email
- Capture: lightweight newsletter + value lead magnet
- Sequence:
  - Day 0: welcome + purpose
  - Day 2: top 3 routines guide
  - Day 5: case-style educational walkthrough
  - Day 8: invite to first consultation

## 2) Weekly Content Cadence
- Monday: "Myth vs reality" post
- Wednesday: "What this symptom pattern may mean" (educational)
- Friday: "7-day habit reset" template
- Sunday: founder build update and user feedback request

## 3) Influencer Outreach Script
Subject: Collaboration idea — educational integrative wellness content

Hi <Name>,
I am building AyuraHealth, an educational wellness platform combining traditional frameworks with responsible AI.
I really liked your content on <topic>.
Would you be open to a small pilot collaboration (1 reel + 1 story + feedback session)?
Happy to share a custom landing page and co-create content that stays medically responsible.

Best,
<Founder Name>

## 4) Practitioner Partnership Funnel
- Build a "Founding Practitioner Circle" page
- Offer:
  - advisory credit
  - co-branded educational content
  - referral visibility
- KPI: onboard first 15 practitioners

## 5) KPI Dashboard (Weekly)
- Organic sessions
- Homepage -> chat click-through rate
- First consultation completion rate
- Free -> trial start rate
- Trial -> paid conversion
- D7 retention
- CAC by channel (if paid starts)
