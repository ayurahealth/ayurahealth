# 🚀 AyuraHealth Growth & Marketing Playbook

This strategy is designed to transform AyuraHealth from a high-fidelity tool into a **High-Authority Wellness Brand**. We focus on building trust through classical citations and scaling through targeted digital awareness.

## 1. The "Authority-First" Ad Strategy
Standard wellness apps sell "feelings." AyuraHealth sells **Verified Wisdom.** 

### 🟢 Meta (Instagram/Facebook) Ad Template
**Visual**: A split screen. Left side shows a dry medical lab report. Right side shows a glowing **AyuraHealth Source Card** citing the *Charaka Samhita*.
**Copy**: 
*"Your blood report says 'Normal.' The Charaka Samhita says 'Imbalance.' VAIDYA bridges 5,000 years of healing wisdom with modern science. Get your integrated action plan today. 🌿"*
**CTA**: [Start Your First Consultation]

### 🟡 Google Search (SEM) Keywords
Target high-intent keywords where users are seeking alternative or integrated answers:
- *"Ayurvedic view on [Condition]"*
- *"TCM acupoints for [Symptom]"*
- *"Integrated medicine for [Bio-marker]"*
- *"Natural healing based on ancient texts"*

---

## 2. Viral Loops: The "Wisdom Share"
We have implemented **Source Cards**. We should make these cards easily sharable.
- **The "Eureka" Moment**: When a user discovers a classical root cause for their symptom, we provide a "Share Wisdom" button. 
- **The Artifact**: An elegant, high-fidelity image of the citation with the AyuraHealth watermark.
- **Conversion**: The shared image includes a QR code/Link: *"Verifed by VAIDYA. Find your root cause."*

---

## 3. Editorial Content: The "NotebookLM" Edge
Since we are vetting our AI Brain with NotebookLM, we can generate a weekly **"Lineage Ledger"** newsletter:
- **Topic**: *"Why the Huangdi Neijing and Charaka Samhita agree on Sleep."*
- **Value**: Establishes AyuraHealth as an educational authority, not just a chatbot.
